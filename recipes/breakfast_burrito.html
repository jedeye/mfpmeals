Breakfast Burrito

Serves 18

Ingredients

18 Santa Maria Flour Tortillas

1000g Kirkland Ground Beef 12% fat

3 large yellow onions, diced

1 tbsp vegetable oil

400g Polpa mutti (tin of tomatoes)

1 tsp salt

1 tsp pepper

1 tsp oregano

8 wedges laughing cow cheese

250g shredded mozarella

Method

Brown mince in a pan until cooked through. Add onions and cook until translucent. Blend tomatoes into passata with salt, pepper and oregano. Cook for another minute. Add cheese wedges, and stir for a couple of minutes until incorporated. Add mozarella, and allow to cool. Weigh and distribute among Burritos and wrap in aluminium foil. Freeze. To reheat, place Burrito on plate and wet a paper towel. Drape towel over Burrito, and microwave on medium for 10 minutes. Toast in a dry pan.
